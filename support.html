Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my AI Plus subscription?

Open System Settings on your Mac, click your Apple ID, then select Subscriptions. Find GetDone Timer and click Cancel Subscription.

Can I use the app on multiple Macs?

You can install the app on multiple Macs with the same Apple ID. However, only one device can be signed into your account at a time. Signing in on a new device will sign out the previous one.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Privacy & Security and click "Export All Data". Your tasks will be saved as a JSON file that you can back up or transfer.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Account page and click "Delete Account" at the bottom. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data from our servers. To also remove local data, use Settings > Privacy & Security > Delete All Data.

What is early bird pricing?

The first 500 users get a special discounted price. Once you purchase at the early bird price, you keep that pricing for all future renewals.

Do I need an API key for the AI Assistant?

Yes. The AI Assistant requires your own API key from OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), or DeepSeek. You can configure it in the AI Assistant settings within the app.

What's the difference between Lifetime and AI Plus?

The Lifetime license is a one-time purchase that unlocks all core app features forever. AI Plus is an annual subscription that provides access to the AI Assistant feature. The Bundle includes both.
