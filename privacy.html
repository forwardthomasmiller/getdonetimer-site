Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 25, 2026

GetDone Timer ("we", "our", or "the App") is a macOS productivity application developed by an independent developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery
  • Display name — optional, shown in the app
  • Authentication tokens — from Apple Sign In or Google Sign In if you choose these methods

1.2 Task Data

Your tasks, timers, pomodoro sessions, and schedule data are stored locally on your device using Core Data. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly use the export feature.

1.3 Usage Statistics

We collect basic usage statistics (e.g., task completion counts, total focus time) to display within the app. These statistics are stored locally and are not transmitted to any server.

1.4 AI Assistant & Third-Party AI Data Sharing

The "Create New Tasks" feature uses third-party AI services to generate task plans. Before any data is sent, the app requires your explicit consent, which you can manage at any time in Settings → AI Data Sharing.

What data is sent to third-party AI providers:

  • Your task descriptions and goals
  • Task preferences (schedule, duration, type)
  • Special instructions you provide
  • Your timezone for scheduling

Who receives the data:

  • DeepSeek (https://api.deepseek.com) — if selected as your AI provider
  • OpenRouter (https://openrouter.ai) — if selected as your AI provider

Data is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you select, using your own API key. GetDone Timer does not relay, store, or log your AI prompts or responses on any of our servers.

Your controls:

  • AI data sharing is off by default and requires your explicit consent
  • You can revoke consent at any time in Settings → AI Data Sharing
  • All core features (timers, tasks, scheduling) work without AI
  • Privacy Mode lets you import tasks via JSON with zero data transmission from the app

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain your account and app functionality
  • To process purchases and manage subscriptions via Apple In-App Purchase
  • To send password reset codes when requested
  • To manage your invitation codes

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign In — for authentication (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign In — for authentication (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple In-App Purchase — for payment processing
  • DeepSeek (optional) — AI task generation, only when you enable AI Data Sharing and select this provider
  • OpenRouter (optional) — AI task generation, only when you enable AI Data Sharing and select this provider

4. Data Storage and Security

Your task data is stored locally on your Mac. Account credentials and API keys are stored securely in the macOS Keychain. Server-side account data is stored on Cloudflare infrastructure with encrypted connections (HTTPS).

5. Data Retention

Your local data remains on your device until you delete it. Server-side account data is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated server-side data is permanently removed.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Export your data — use the Export feature in Settings > Privacy & Security
  • Delete your local data — use the Delete All Data feature in Settings > Privacy & Security
  • Delete your account — use the Delete Account option in the Account page, which permanently removes all server-side data

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
